# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone interacting with Distro.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Use inclusive language and assume positive intent.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or disruptive behavior.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Reporting
If you observe unacceptable behavior, please open a `Code of Conduct` issue or contact the maintainers privately. Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from the community spaces.

Thank you for helping keep Distro a positive environment.
